Remembering Mike’s Life

Obituary

A Life of Faith, Service, and Love for People

Mike was dearly loved by his family and by many friends who appreciated his warmth, humor, and sincere interest in others.

Merritt Edward Walker, known to family and friends as Mike, passed away on April 28, 2026, at the age of 79. He was born on January 4, 1947, in Springfield, Missouri, to Merritt and Kathleen Walker.

Mike married his wife, Vickie, on April 26, 1969. Their story began long before that, since they were raised next door to each other and knew each other for many years. Together, they built a life filled with family, faith, friendship, and many shared memories.

Mike will be remembered for his love for people and for his efforts to help others learn more about the future for mankind as shown in the Bible. He was baptized as one of Jehovah's Witnesses on May 13, 1973. For many years, he served faithfully as an elder and regular pioneer. He loved the ministry and especially enjoyed helping new ones come to know Jehovah.

Mike is survived by his beloved wife, Vickie Walker; his daughter, Andraeh Walker of Murfreesboro, Tennessee; and his sister, Sharon Walker of Raytown, Missouri. He was dearly loved by his family and by many friends who appreciated his warmth, humor, and sincere interest in others.

Mike enjoyed having fun with his friends. He liked playing cards, hiking, biking, water sports, music, traveling, and staying active. He also enjoyed learning about different cultures. One adventure that was especially meaningful to him was his trip to Ecuador, South America, where he was able to share the Bible's message with others.

Mike's family finds comfort in Jesus' promise at John 5:28, 29, and they look forward to seeing him again in the resurrection, restored to good health and ready to go to work.
